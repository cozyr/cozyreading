Percy Jackson and the Olympians

by Rick Riordan

The Lightning Thief

Twelve-year-old Percy Jackson is on the most dangerous quest of his life. With the help of a satyr and a daughter of Athena, Percy must journey across the United States to recover Zeus’ stolen master bolt. Along the way, Percy faces mythological enemies, family secrets, and an Oracle’s warning about betrayal.

Book 1

The Sea of Monsters

When the camp’s protective borders begin to fail, Percy and his friends race against time to find a powerful cure. Their quest takes them into the Sea of Monsters—where danger, rescue, and a shocking family truth await.

Book 2

The Titan's Curse

Percy joins a rescue mission that leads to a missing goddess, a terrifying monster, and a prophecy that changes everything. The Titan lord Kronos’ plans grow darker—and the stakes get higher.

Book 3

The Battle of the Labyrinth

With Kronos’ forces preparing to invade, Percy and his friends must navigate the treacherous Labyrinth—an underground maze full of traps, monsters, and shifting paths.
